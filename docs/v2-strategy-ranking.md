# V2 Strategy Ranking

Ranking evaluates research candidates after courtroom review. The policy scores out-of-sample expectancy, stability, robustness, cost resilience, regime diversity, evidence freshness, and similarity support while penalizing drawdown, tail risk, complexity, turnover, stale evidence, and fragile costs.

Court-rejected strategies and candidates with missing lineage are blocked from focused research. Ranking emits research-governance decisions only and cannot activate execution or publish signals.
